Workshop - Project

In this project, I am creating a workshop registration system using C#. Users will be able to sign up for various workshops by indicating their top three preferences. Based on their input, the user will be assigned to a workshop. At the end of the process, a list of workshops along with their assigned participants will be displayed. The system will be designed to handle multiple workshops and participants efficiently. Users will have the flexibility to select their preferred workshops, allowing for a personalized experience.

The registration process will ensure that participants are allocated to workshops based on their preferences. The output will provide a comprehensive overview of the workshops and the participants assigned to each one. This will help organizers manage and track workshop attendance effectively. The project aims to streamline the workshop registration process, making it convenient for both participants and administrators. By utilizing C#, we will implement robust functionalities to ensure the system is reliable and user-friendly. The project will involve handling data input, processing, and output in a structured manner. Overall, the goal is to create a seamless workshop registration system that meets the needs of both organizers and participants.
